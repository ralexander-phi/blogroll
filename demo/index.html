A fresh look at blogrolls.

  • That's a blogroll, over there.
  • Blogrolls are a common feature in early websites. A list of blogs you follow. A checklist of places to look at. Advertising our web friendships. Blogrolls were the beginning of today's social web.
  • It's time to take a fresh look at the humble blogroll.
  • Some of the new features:
    • Real-time updates.
    • List sorted by name or time.
    • Expand a blog to see recent posts.
    • See when each blog was updated.
    • Keyboard navigation.
  • This list is a set of interesting blogs hosted by Automattic, and a few others I've added.
  • When this product is ready, you will be able to supply your own list, and display it anywhere, it's just a blogroll.
  • And even better we can use it to bootstrap a new kind of social network! 😀
  • Dave Winer, March 2024.
